About Daybook.Cloud

Daybook.Cloud is built to make bookkeeping, invoicing, ledgers, branches, financial years, and reports easier for growing businesses.

Built for practical finance work

Daybook.Cloud focuses on the daily finance operations that businesses need to keep moving: recording entries, managing ledgers, creating invoices, reviewing branch activity, and preparing useful reports.

The product is developed by Tociva Private Limited, based in Kerala, India. Tociva builds practical software products for business operations, finance workflows, and modern web applications.

What guides the product

Simplicity

Bookkeeping software should make daily finance work clearer, not harder.

Structure

Ledgers, branches, financial years, and reports should follow a consistent operating model.

Business usefulness

The product should help owners, accountants, and operators understand the numbers they work with.

Who Daybook.Cloud helps

Daybook.Cloud is intended for businesses that need finance records to be more organized and easier to review.

Small businesses

Manage daily entries, invoices, ledgers, and reports without depending on disconnected spreadsheets.

Branch businesses

Track finance activity across branches while keeping company-level visibility.

Accountants and operators

Use a practical workspace for routine bookkeeping and finance review.

Owners and managers

Understand business movement through clearer records and reports.
